Luis Jeronimo ABOUT ME

I am Luis Enrique Jeronimo Martinez, I'm a backend developer, machine learning enthusiast and learning a little about frontend and devops, I studied Computer Systems Engineering and a Master's degree in CS in Mexico. Actually I'm working at IBM and Citibanamex in Mexico City creating REST APIs related to transactions, investments and personal banking, it's interesting to know how a bank works. For this I use Java and Spring-Boot among other technologies. In my day-to-day I work under Agile manifest and Scrum framework to manage my deliverables and my time, I support my job using Jira and Confluence to share my status and knowledge (documentation about projects I made).

Before IBM, I went to Bogotá, Colombia to made a research in Machine Learning with a PhD professor, it was a wonderful experience because I learned a lot from another culture and I pushed myself into a big challenge to learn about ML, Python, algorithms and read a lot of papers published in some digital libraries, I consider myself as an autodidact guy, but this experience helped me a lot to growth my learning skills and discover my own learning method. I published a paper on IEEE :D

And before all this great experiences, during my university I went to a lot of contests of different categories such as algorithms, entrepreneurship, software development and hackathons.
